A brief about Arvind Bhat

Arvind Bhat is a former International badminton player. He has represented India in almost all major events around the world. He was part of India team from 2001 to 2015. Arvind has a mechanical engineering degree from one of the most presitigious colleges in Bangalore, Karnataka called UVCE college.
