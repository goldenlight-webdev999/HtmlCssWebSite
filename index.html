We are level up

We provide world-class badminton coaching in an inclusive environment that empowers students to enjoy and compete at their full potential. Whether you are a beginner, advanced player, or somewhere in between, we have programs for you.

Disciplined Training
Success in sport requires discipline. We strive to embrace and help our athletes get results with an emphasis on discipline in all our coaching programs for all levels.
Excellent Infrastructure
Our state of the art training facility consists of 12 excellent wooden courts with professional Li-Ning court mats to reduce injury and increase performance.
Advanced Fitness
Badminton is a fitness intensive sport. Our in-house trainers and award winning physios tailor fitness programs to help our athletes stay fighting fit.
